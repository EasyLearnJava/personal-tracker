## â“ FAQ

**Q: What if I make a payment but forget to select the bank account?**
A: The payment is still recorded, but the bank account won't be updated. You can manually update it later.

**Q: Can I edit a payment after recording it?**
A: Yes, go to Payment History, find the payment, and click Edit (coming soon).

**Q: What if I add an expense to the wrong card?**
A: The debt will increase for that card. You can manually adjust the debt balance or delete the expense and recreate it.

**Q: How do I track multiple credit cards?**
A: Create a separate debt for each credit card. When you add expenses, select the correct card.

**Q: Can I see payment history for a specific date range?**
A: Yes, use the date filters in Payment History view.
